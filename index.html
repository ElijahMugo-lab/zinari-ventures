From cherry to cup.

We oversee the full chain, wet mill to dry mill to packaging, using the washed process Kenya is celebrated for.

Cherry harvest

Ripe red cherries are hand-picked by co-op member farmers during the main crop (October to December) and fly crop (April to June), at 1,400 to 2,000m.

Wet milling

Cherries are pulped at the washing station within hours of picking. The mucilage is fermented off and washed away with clean water, preserving the beans' clarity.

Drying

Parchment coffee dries on raised beds over two to three weeks, turned regularly for even moisture reduction to 11 or 12 percent.

Dry milling and grading

Hulling, grading by screen size, density sorting and colour sorting produce clean, export-standard green beans.

Roasting and packaging

We roast to profile and pack in valve-sealed bags with full traceability: farm, co-op, region, grade and roast date.

AA
Screen 17 to 18, premium

The largest bean size and the most sought-after internationally. Full-bodied, with complex acidity and pronounced blackcurrant notes. Our primary export grade.

AB Screen 15 to 16, standard

A combination of A and B screens, equally complex and true to Kenyan terroir. Excellent for cafe supply and the local market.

PB Peaberry

A single round bean per cherry, prized for concentrated sweetness and a bright, lively cup.

Where we come from.

Zinari Ventures was born from a simple observation: Kenya grows some of the finest produce and coffee in the world, yet growers often receive too little of its value. Supply chains are long, middlemen are many, and the farmer at the end of the chain is often the last to benefit.

We built Zinari to change that model. We entered commercial agreements with cooperatives like the Wachukuzi Planters Union to process, package and sell their coffee directly, and built direct relationships with farms across Kenya, from Meru to Naivasha, from Thika to Western Kenya.

We also run our own farm and poultry unit. This is not theory for us; we are farmers too. We understand the inputs, the cycles, the risks and the rewards, and that is what makes our consulting different. Today Zinari Ventures is a proud subsidiary of Robin Investments Group Inc.
